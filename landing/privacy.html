Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data Collection

Astryxion API collects minimal personal data to provide our services. This includes:

  • Personal Info: E-mail addresses and IP addresses for account authentication and security.
  • Usage Data: Number of API requests, response codes, and latency for billing and performance monitoring.
  • Esoteric Context: We do NOT store individual birth data (names, locations, times) sent to the API, except for caching purposes defined in our technical limits (typically 24 hours).

2. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS for data in transit. All API keys are hashed (HMAC-SHA256) to ensure they cannot be recovered by unauthorized parties.

3. Third-party Services

To provide high-scale celestial calculations, we use:

  • Redis Cloud: For ultra-fast session and rate-limit storage.
  • Google Cloud Platform: For compute and API infrastructure.
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing (Professional/Enterprise tiers).

4. Cookies & Tokens

We use session cookies in the Developer Portal to maintain your login session. We do NOT use tracking cookies for advertising or non-functional purposes.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and LGPD, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To request account deletion, please contact us at support@astryxion.com. Your API keys and usage history will be permanently erased.
